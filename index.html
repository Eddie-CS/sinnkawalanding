About Us关于我们

SINNKAWA— where Singapore Meets Cuteness!SINNKAWA— 新加坡遇见可爱的地方！

Born in the heart of Singapore, Sinnkawa reimagines local culture through a playful lens. Our original characters – from the crown-wearing Merlion “Morio” to the bubbly Otata – each tell a story inspired by the city’s spirit.心川诞生于新加坡，以充满玩味的视角重新诠释本地文化。我们的原创角色——头戴皇冠的鱼尾狮“Morio”和活力四射的Otata——都讲述着这座城市的精神故事。

More than a brand, Sinnkawa is a feel-good lifestyle infused with charm and imagination. We don’t just make souvenirs; we bring smiles and spark joy through every design, story, and product.SINNKAWA不仅仅是一个品牌，更是一种注入魅力与想象力的美好生活方式。我们不只制作纪念品，更希望通过每个设计、故事和产品带来笑容和喜悦。
